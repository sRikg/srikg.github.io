My name is Srik, short for Srikanth.

I started as an amateur math nerd who is passionate about delving into various topics and their origins. I've always had an innate curiosity to question everything, and this curiosity led me to explore the vast and exciting world of AI and Data Science.
I remember getting my first personal computer at the age of thirteen, and the allure of doing anything imaginable on a screen quickly hooked me. Since then, I've been actively seeking new ways to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies. From subscribing to various websites and following renowned people whose work amazes me, I always keep an eye out for exciting new discoveries and innovations.

I try to learn many opinions for the same issue, some with which I agree, some with which I disagree, and some with which I couldn't even comprehend.
